### Signs: Core Qualities
**Modalities:**
- Cardinal (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn): initiates, leads, starts
- Fixed (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius): sustains, stabilizes, resists change
- Mutable (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces): adapts, transitions, distributes

**Elements:**
- Fire (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius): spirit, enthusiasm, identity, inspiration
- Earth (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn): matter, body, practicality, resources
- Air (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius): mind, relationship, ideas, communication
- Water (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces): soul, emotion, intuition, depth

## Aspects: How Planets Talk to Each Other

| Aspect | Degrees | Orb | Quality |
|--------|---------|-----|---------|
| Conjunction | 0° | ±8° | Fusion — amplifies, can overwhelm |
| Opposition | 180° | ±8° | Tension requiring integration — awareness through conflict |
| Trine | 120° | ±6° | Flow and ease — gifts, often taken for granted |
| Square | 90° | ±6° | Friction and drive — challenges that build character |
| Sextile | 60° | ±4° | Opportunity — requires effort to activate |
| Quincunx/Inconjunct | 150° | ±2° | Adjustment required — incompatible energies, chronic restlessness |
| Semi-square | 45° | ±2° | Irritation, minor friction |

**Rules:**
- Tight orbs (within 2°): extremely potent, life-defining
- Applying aspects (planets moving toward exact): building tension/energy
- Separating aspects (planets moving away from exact): peak has passed, integrating

**Aspect Patterns:**
- **T-Square**: Two planets in opposition, both squared by a third. High tension, high achievement drive. The apex planet is the outlet.
- **Grand Trine**: Three planets in trine (same element). Enormous ease in that element — but can indicate complacency or insularity.
- **Grand Cross**: Four planets in two oppositions + four squares. Intense, multi-directional tension. High difficulty, high capability.
- **Yod (Finger of God)**: Two planets in sextile, both quincunx a third. Fated, uncomfortable, requires radical adjustment. The apex planet feels like destiny pressing.
- **Stellium**: Three or more planets in same sign or house. Massive concentration of energy there — can dominate entire chart.

## Natal Chart Interpretation Order

1. **Chart shape** (bowl, bucket, locomotive, splash, bundle) — overall life orientation
2. **Ascendant + chart ruler** — the filter through which everything expresses
3. **Sun + house** — core identity and where it wants to shine
4. **Moon + house + aspects** — emotional needs, habitual responses
5. **Stelliums** — concentrated life themes
6. **Angular planets** — loudest influences
7. **Saturn** — where you must work hardest, also your greatest achievement
8. **North Node** — soul direction, often feels unfamiliar or challenging
9. **South Node** — comfort zone, past life residue
10. **Aspect patterns** (T-squares, yods, grand trines)
11. **Individual placements** in context of the above

## Transits

**What transits are:** Current planetary positions activating natal chart degrees.

**Most significant transits:**
- **Saturn transits** to personal planets: 2-3 year restructuring, tests, maturation
- **Pluto transits**: years-long, irreversible transformation
- **Uranus transits**: sudden disruption, liberation, awakening
- **Jupiter transits**: 1-year expansion windows — opportunities, but also excess
- **Saturn return** (ages ~29, ~58): major life audit and identity reset
- **Chiron return** (~age 50): deep healing, integration of wound
- **Nodal return** (~every 18 years): fate recalibration

**Orbs for transits:**
- Slow planets (Pluto, Neptune, Uranus): 3° applying, 1° separating
- Saturn, Jupiter: 2° applying, 1° separating
- Faster planets (Mars, Venus, Mercury): 1° orb only — short-lived

## Synastry (Compatibility)

**Key overlays to check:**
- Partner's planets in your 1st/5th/7th/8th houses: strong attraction
- Partner's planets conjunct your Sun/Moon/Venus/Mars/Ascendant: magnetic connection
- Partner's Saturn on your personal planets: karmic, stabilizing, sometimes restrictive

**Key aspects between charts:**
- Venus-Mars aspects: romantic and sexual chemistry
- Sun-Moon aspects: emotional compatibility, complementary roles
- Moon-Moon aspects: emotional resonance or friction
- Saturn aspects: longevity but also restriction — check if it's burden or structure
- North Node contacts: fated encounters, growth-oriented

**Composite chart:** Midpoint chart of two people — describes the relationship as its own entity. More important for long-term partnership than synastry overlays.

**Red flags (not dealbreakers, but require awareness):**
- Double whammy Saturn-Venus hard aspects: love that feels like work
- Moon square/opposite Moon: deeply different emotional needs
- Mars square/opposite Mars: ongoing conflict, also high passion
